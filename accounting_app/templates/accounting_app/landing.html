📌 About Us

Who We Are

Nkula Water Users Association (WUA) is a community-based institution established in 2013 under the Government of Malawi with financial support from the African Development Bank (ADB). Our purpose is to empower communities to take full responsibility in water management and support community development initiatives.

We work with local leaders and community members to ensure water schemes are properly managed, protected, and maintained for the benefit of all.

🎯 Vision, Mission & Values

Our guiding principles
